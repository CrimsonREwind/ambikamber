About Director

Discover the creative journey of Esha Verma, the visionary behind Ambikamber. Hailing from the vibrant city of Lucknow, Esha's artistic odyssey began in her childhood, where her love for colors and creativity blossomed. Her educational voyage took her from local roots to prestigious institutions: a diploma in textile design from Banaras, followed by BSc and MBA in fashion and technology from Delhi.

Founded on April 28, 2022, Ambikamber embodies Esha's passion for artistry and innovation. Specializing in bespoke nameplates, each piece reflects her distinctive touch, crafted with love and precision. Beyond her entrepreneurial endeavors, Esha extends her artistic flair globally, teaching young minds the joys of art through online classes.

For Esha, every day is a canvas for new ideas and inspiration. Her dedication to merging creativity with education fuels her spirit, making each endeavor at Ambikamber a testament to her boundless enthusiasm. Born and raised in Lucknow, Esha Verma, at 25, continues to color the world with her passion, embracing each moment with joy and purpose.
